Our Story

Founded in 2022, Bhumika Cakes started as a small bakery with a big dream — to create extraordinary cakes that taste as good as they look. Over the years, we’ve grown into a beloved destination for dessert lovers, known for our attention to detail and unparalleled flavors.

Whether you're craving a classic chocolate cake, a personalized creation, or seasonal delights, we promise to make it with love and care.
